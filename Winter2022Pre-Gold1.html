Pre-Gold 1 Sprint (Apr. 4, 2022 - Apr. 10, 2022)

Pre-Gold 1: 9.5 Total Work Hours

*Apr. 4th - Monday: 0.5 Hours
I communicated with Spartasoft President and Spartasoft Studio Producer about a connection opportunity. I also saw their Spartasoft page and followed it (gotta keep the rivalry up!).

*Apr. 5th - Tuesday: 1.25 Hours
From 11:30am - 12:00pm EST I connected Spartasoft President and Spartasoft Studio Producer with Full-Sail University graduate Game Designer who also went to MSU.
From 7:00pm - 7:45pm EST I attended the Matt Rader playtest session for WolverineSoft Studio.

*Apr. 6th - Wednesday: N/A

*Apr. 7th - Thursday: 3 Hours Total
From 7:00pm - 9:00pm EST I attended the WolverineSoft general meeting. We were honored to hear Austin Yarger's new XR game-dev course prioritizing Unreal Engine and Perforce with more focus on social impacts than its Unity raw game-dev counterpart course that currently exists. Looks interesting! Additionally, we went over historic games in WolverineSoft's history which started in 1999. Some projects I've been involved with/witnessed firsthand were spoken about! It was also super cool to finally see the renowned Crisis Wolverine game! Additionally afterwards I attended the WolverineSoft officer meeting.
From 11:00pm - 12:00am EST I networked with some individuals to share the word more about our playtest list and to recruit someone from UC Berkeley for Bluebird Studios.

*Apr. 8th - Friday: N/A

*Apr. 9th - Saturday: 2.25 Hours Total
From 12:00pm - 2:15pm EST I created our Resume Book Consent form, asking for feedback from the Studio directors and our Advisor Austin Yarger for feedback. I told this news to several channels, and created announcement explaining what exactly a Resume Book is. I was careful in how I worded this so as to get form results quickly while explaining why we need it in a way so as to encourage people to take it rather than be suspicious of it (like "why would I provide my resume for this?"" type of thing).

*Apr. 10th - Sunday: 2.5 Hours Total
From 4:00pm - 6:45pm EST I applied marketing and game-design skills I keep learning from WolverineSoft Studio with the Bluebird Studios project. We discussed several designs for the upcoming game, and afterwards I created a brief playtest demographics doc and then reached out to MPowered Entrepreneurship about it. If there was any other experiences of personal game-dev growth/WolverineSoft Studio work time this day I did not document it nor do I remember it.
